The Best Shipping Container Conversions - Made in Scunthorpe

Engage

The UK's No. 1 Shipping Container Conversion Specialists

Workshops

Shipping containers can be transformed into durable, secure workshops ideal for on-site work or small businesses. They offer weatherproof environments, easy customization (e.g. workbenches, tool storage), and mobility for remote or temporary setups.

Offices

Container offices provide a compact, cost-effective alternative to traditional office buildings. They can be outfitted with insulation, power, air conditioning, and internet, making them suitable for both temporary site offices and long-term use.

Canteens

Converted containers make ideal on-site canteens, offering a quick-to-deploy dining area for workers or guests. They can include kitchen equipment, seating, HVAC systems, and hygiene facilities in a self-contained, transportable unit.

Bars

Container bars are a popular option for festivals, events, and seasonal venues. Their industrial look is trendy, and they can be fitted with serving hatches, refrigeration, sinks, and stylish interiors, offering both functionality and flair.

Retail Popup Shops

Containers provide a modern, flexible solution for pop-up retail. Brands can customize them to reflect their identity, and their mobility allows easy relocation between events or sites. They’re great for short-term campaigns or testing new markets.

Garden Rooms

These containers are converted into stylish, private spaces used as home offices, studios, or relaxation areas. With insulation, large windows, and cladding, they blend into garden environments while adding extra usable space.

Homes

Shipping container homes are a sustainable, modular housing solution. With insulation, plumbing, electrical systems, and creative design, multiple containers can be combined to form anything from tiny homes to multi-room dwellings.

Modular Offices

Using multiple containers, modular offices offer scalable office space with a professional look. They’re ideal for growing businesses needing fast, customizable, and relocatable premises with meeting rooms, reception areas, and open-plan layouts.

Pools

Containers can be converted into swimming pools with reinforced walls, filtration systems, and decking. They are a cost-effective, above-ground pool option that can be transported, quickly installed, and even relocated if needed.

Gyms

Container gyms provide mobile fitness spaces that can be outfitted with strength and cardio equipment, ventilation, mirrors, and flooring. They’re great for personal trainers, boot camps, or corporate wellness solutions.

Toliet Facilities

These conversions create robust, hygienic restroom facilities for events, sites, or remote locations. They can include male/female toilets, showers, disabled access, and eco-friendly features such as water-saving fixtures.

Micro Brewaries

Containers are increasingly used for compact microbrewery setups, providing controlled environments for brewing, fermenting, and serving beer. They’re ideal for small-scale production, with the added benefit of being mobile and expandable.
